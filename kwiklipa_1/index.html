About Kwiklipa

Kwiklipa is a mobile self-checkout solution that offers the best online checkout for physical stores.It offers convenience to shoppers who can now go to the store,scan products ,pay and leave.Shoppers can also create a shopping list from anywhere,pay for their intended goods and later come to the store and pick them

KwikLipa FAQ

We got you coverd, check those faq if its not there just ask us.

What is kwikpay ?
  • kwikpay is an application that enables you to quiclky, easily and securely pay from smartphones in the supermarket.
How does one create a shopping list on kwiklipa?

Tap or click on the app and log in ,choose a store that you want to shop in and make a shopping list by clicking on the items you want to buy

How do i know my order is ready for pick up?

You will receive a notification from the shop, alerting you on

Does Kwiklipa offer delivery services?

No. Kindly arrange to pick up items ordered or inform the shop of your pick up arrangements.

How does one scan an item using kwiklipa app?

Open the app in store.Hold your phone over the barcode or QR code to scan ,once you are done you will receive an e-receipt of the total cost.

How do i pay on kwiklipa?

After creating a shopping list or scanning your desired products the payment option is indicated just below the total cost ,click on it and the various payment options (mpesa,debit/ credit card) will appear ,click on one and pay the required amount

What are the two shopping options using kwiklipa?

When using kwiklipa you can either choose the order,pay & pick up option which involves selecting products from a shop in the app and picking up your products when they are ready . The second option is to go to the store physically ,scan the chosen products and pay for them then leave without queueing.
